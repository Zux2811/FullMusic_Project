# Theme Selection Feature - User Guide

## Overview

The Music App now features a comprehensive theme selection system with two beautiful themes: **Light Theme** and **Dark Theme**. Users can choose their preferred theme during sign-up and change it anytime from settings.

### For New Users (First-Time Setup)

1. **Sign Up**
   - Fill in username, email, and password
   - Click "Sign Up" button

2. **Choose Your Theme**
   - You'll be taken to the Theme Selection Page
   - Choose between Light Theme or Dark Theme
   - Click on your preferred theme

3. **Enjoy!**
   - You're now in the Home Page with your selected theme
   - Your theme preference is automatically saved

### For Existing Users

1. **Open Settings**
   - Go to Home Page
   - Tap the "Settings" tab at the bottom

2. **Change Theme**
   - In the "Appearance" section, select your preferred theme
   - Light Theme or Dark Theme
   - Changes apply immediately
